Meet Our Founder

Dr. Kamal Yadav

Founder & Chief Dental Officer

Dr. Kamal Yadav is a renowned dentist and the visionary founder of RBS Hospital, Gurgaon. With years of experience in dentistry, he specializes in providing advanced dental care, including cosmetic procedures, restorative treatments, and preventive care.

As the driving force behind RBS Hospital, Dr. Yadav has created a state-of-the-art healthcare facility dedicated to delivering compassionate and high-quality medical services. His commitment to patient well-being, combined with a focus on innovation and excellence, has made RBS Hospital a trusted name in healthcare.

Dr. Kamal Yadav's leadership ensures that every patient receives personalized care, making RBS Hospital a center of excellence in Gurgaon.

Our Mission

Committed to Your Health & Happiness

RBS Hospital, Gurgaon, is a trusted healthcare center dedicated to providing high-quality medical care with compassion and advanced technology. We are here to serve our community with modern facilities and a focus on putting patients first.

Our team of expert doctors, caring nurses, and friendly staff ensures every patient receives the best care possible. With over 50 specialized departments, we offer many services — including heart care, bone care, child care, and more — all under one roof.

Whether you need a routine check-up, a surgery, or advanced tests, we are here to guide you every step of the way. At RBS Hospital, your health and happiness are always our priority.
